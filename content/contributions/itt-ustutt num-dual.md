`num-dual` is a Rust library that provides generalized, recursive, scalar and vector (hyper) dual numbers for the automatic and exact calculation of (partial) derivatives.

I have been looking into it for [a curve research](https://github.com/RagibHasin/try_hyperbez) and needed quadrant-preserving arctangent (`atan2`) calculation for second order dual numbers and just added that.

Understory is a small family of crates designed to be combined in different stacks over time,
that provides foundational spatial and scene data structures for user interfaces, graphics editors, and CAD viewers.

My contribution was to the `understory_virtual_list` crate that provides abstractions for 1D virtualization primitives, in particular, adding support for sparse lazy loading of list items.
